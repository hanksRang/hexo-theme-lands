<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">下马饮君酒，问君何所之？</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">君言不得意，归卧南山陲。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">但去莫复问，白云无尽时。</p>

</div>
