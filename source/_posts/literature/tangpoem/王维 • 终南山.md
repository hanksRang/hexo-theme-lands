<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">太乙近天都，连山接海隅。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">白云回望合，青霭入看无。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">分野中峰变，阴晴众壑殊。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欲投人处宿，隔水问樵夫。</p>

</div>
