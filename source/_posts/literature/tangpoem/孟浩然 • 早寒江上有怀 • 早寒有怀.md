<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟浩然
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">木落雁南度，北风江上寒。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">我家襄水曲，遥隔楚云端。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">乡泪客中尽，孤帆天际看。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">迷津欲有问，平海夕漫漫。</p>

</div>
