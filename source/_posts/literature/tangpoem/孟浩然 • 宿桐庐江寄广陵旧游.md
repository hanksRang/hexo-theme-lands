<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟浩然
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">山暝闻猿愁，沧江急夜流。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">风鸣两岸叶，月照一孤舟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">建德非吾土，维扬忆旧游。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">还将两行泪，遥寄海西头。</p>

</div>
