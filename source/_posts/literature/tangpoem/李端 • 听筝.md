<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李端
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">鸣筝金粟柱，素手玉房前。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欲得周郎顾，时时误拂弦。</p>
