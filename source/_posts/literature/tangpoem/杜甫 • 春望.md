<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">国破山河在，城春草木深。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">感时花溅泪，恨别鸟惊心。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">烽火连三月，家书抵万金。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">白头搔更短，浑欲不胜簪。</p>

</div>
