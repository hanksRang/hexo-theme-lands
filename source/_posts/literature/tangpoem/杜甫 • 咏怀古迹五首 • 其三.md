<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">群山万壑赴荆门，生长明妃尚有村。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">一去紫台连朔漠，独留青冢向黄昏。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">画图省识春风面，环佩空归夜月魂。(夜月 一作：月夜 环</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">佩</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">一作：环</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">珮</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">)</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">千载琵琶作胡语，分明怨恨曲中论。</p>

</div>
