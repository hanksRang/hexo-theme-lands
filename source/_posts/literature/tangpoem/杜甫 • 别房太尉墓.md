<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">他乡复行役，驻马别孤坟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">近泪无干土，低空有断云。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">对棋陪谢傅，把剑觅徐君。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">唯见林花落，莺啼送客闻。</p>

</div>
