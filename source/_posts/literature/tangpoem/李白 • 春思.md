<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">燕草如碧丝，秦桑低绿枝。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">当君怀归日，是妾断肠时。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">春风不相识，何事入罗帏。</p>

</div>
