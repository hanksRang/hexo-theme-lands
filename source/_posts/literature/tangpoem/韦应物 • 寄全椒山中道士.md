<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 韦应物
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">今朝郡斋冷，忽念山中客。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">涧底束荆薪，归来煮白石。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欲持一瓢酒，远慰风雨夕。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">落叶满空山，何处寻行迹。</p>

</div>
