<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 刘长卿
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">一路经行处，莓苔见履痕。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">白云依静渚，春草闭闲门。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">过雨看松色，随山到水源。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">溪花与禅意，相对亦忘言。</p>

</div>
