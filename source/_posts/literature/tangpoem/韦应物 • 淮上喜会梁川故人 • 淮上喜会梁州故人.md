<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 韦应物
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">江汉曾为客，相逢每醉还。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">浮云一别后，流水十年间。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欢笑情如旧，萧疏鬓已斑。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">何因不归去？淮上有秋山。</p>

</div>
