<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王湾
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">客路青山外，行舟绿水前。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">潮平两岸阔，风正一帆悬。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">海日生残夜，江春入旧年。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">乡书何处达？归雁洛阳边。</p>

</div>
