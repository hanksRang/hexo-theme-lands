<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">镜湖三百里，菡萏发荷花。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">五月西施采，人看隘若耶。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">回舟不待月，归去越王家。</p>

</div>
