<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">青山横北郭，白水绕东城。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">此地一为别，孤蓬万里征。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">浮云游子意，落日故人情。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">挥手自兹去，萧萧班马鸣。</p>

</div>
