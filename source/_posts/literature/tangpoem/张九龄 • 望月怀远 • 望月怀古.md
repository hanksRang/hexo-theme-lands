<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张九龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">海上生明月，天涯共此时。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">情人怨遥夜，竟夕起相思。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">灭烛怜光满，披衣觉露滋。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">不堪盈手赠，还寝梦佳期。</p>

</div>
