<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟浩然
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">夕阳度西岭，群壑倏已暝。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">松月生夜凉，风泉满清听。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">樵人归欲尽，烟鸟栖初定。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">之子期宿来，孤琴候萝径。</p>

</div>
