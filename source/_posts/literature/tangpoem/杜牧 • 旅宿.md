<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜牧
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">旅馆无良伴，凝情自悄然。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">寒灯思旧事，断雁警愁眠。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">远梦归侵晓，家书到隔年。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">沧江好烟月，门系钓鱼船。</p>

</div>
