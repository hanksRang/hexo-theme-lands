<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">空山新雨后，天气晚来秋。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">明月松间照，清泉石上流。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">竹喧归浣女，莲动下渔舟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">随意春芳歇，王孙自可留。</p>

</div>
