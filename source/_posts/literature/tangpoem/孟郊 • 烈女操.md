<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟郊
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">梧桐相待老，鸳鸯会双死。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">贞女贵殉夫，舍生亦如此。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">波澜誓不起，妾心古井水。</p>

</div>
