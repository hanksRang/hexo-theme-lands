<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 韩翃
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">长簟迎风早，空城澹月华。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">星河秋一雁，砧杵夜千家。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">节候看应晚，心期卧亦赊。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">向来吟秀句，不觉已鸣鸦。</p>

</div>
