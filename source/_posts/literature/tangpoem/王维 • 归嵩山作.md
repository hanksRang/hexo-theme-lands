<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">清川带长薄，车马去闲闲。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">流水如有意，暮禽相与还。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">荒城临古渡，落日满秋山。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">迢递嵩高下，归来且闭关。</p>

</div>
