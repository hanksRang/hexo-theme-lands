<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟浩然
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">故人具鸡黍，邀我至田家。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">绿树村边合，青山郭外斜。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">开轩面场圃，把酒话桑麻。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">待到重阳日，还来就菊花。</p>

</div>
