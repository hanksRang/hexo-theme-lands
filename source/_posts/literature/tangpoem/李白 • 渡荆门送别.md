<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">渡远荆门外，来从楚国游。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">山随平野尽，江入大荒流。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">月下飞天镜，云生结海楼。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">仍怜故乡水，万里送行舟。</p>

</div>
