<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 刘长卿
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">望君烟水阔，挥手泪沾巾。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">飞鸟没何处，青山空向人。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">长江一帆远，落日五湖春。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">谁见汀洲上，相思愁白蘋。</p>

</div>
