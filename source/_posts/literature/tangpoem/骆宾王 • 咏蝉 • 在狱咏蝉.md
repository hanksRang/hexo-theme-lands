<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 骆宾王
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">西陆蝉声唱，南冠客思深。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">不堪玄鬓影，来对白头吟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">露重飞难进，风多响易沉。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">无人信高洁，谁为表予心？</p>

</div>
