<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">斜阳照墟落，穷巷牛羊归。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">野老念牧童，倚杖候荆扉。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">雉雊麦苗秀，蚕眠桑叶稀。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">田夫荷锄至，相见语依依。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">即此羡闲逸，怅然吟式微。</p>

</div>
