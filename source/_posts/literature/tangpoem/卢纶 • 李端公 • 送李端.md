<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 卢纶
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">故关衰草遍，离别自堪悲。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">路出寒云外，人归暮雪时。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">少孤为客早，多难识君迟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">掩泪空相向，风尘何处期。</p>

</div>
