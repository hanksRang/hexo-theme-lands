<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张九龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">江南有丹橘，经冬犹绿林。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">岂伊地气暖？自有岁寒心。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">可以荐嘉客，奈何阻重深。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">运命惟所遇，循环不可寻。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">徒言树桃李，此木岂无阴？</p>

</div>
