<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">花隐掖垣暮，啾啾栖鸟过。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">星临万户动，月傍九霄多。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">不寝听金钥，因风想玉珂。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">明朝有封事，数问夜如何。</p>

</div>
