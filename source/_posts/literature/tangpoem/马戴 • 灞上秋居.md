<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 马戴
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">灞原风雨定，晚见雁行频。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">落叶他乡树，寒灯独夜人。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">空园白露滴，孤壁野僧邻。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">寄卧郊扉久，何年致此身。</p>

</div>
