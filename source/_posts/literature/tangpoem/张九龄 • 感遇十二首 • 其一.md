<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张九龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">兰叶春葳蕤，桂华秋皎洁。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欣欣此生意，自尔为佳节。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">谁知林栖者，闻风坐相悦。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">草木有本心，何求美人折！</p>

</div>
