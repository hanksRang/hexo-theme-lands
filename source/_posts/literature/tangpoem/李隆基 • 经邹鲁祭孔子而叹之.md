<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李隆基
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">夫子何为者，栖栖一代中。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">地犹鄹氏邑，宅即鲁王宫。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">叹凤嗟身否，伤麟怨道穷。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">今看两楹奠，当与梦时同。</p>

</div>
