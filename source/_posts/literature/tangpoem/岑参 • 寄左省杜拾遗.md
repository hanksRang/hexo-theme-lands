<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 岑参
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">联步趋丹陛，分曹限紫微。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">晓随天仗入，暮惹御香归。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">白发悲花落，青云羡鸟飞。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">圣朝无阙事，自觉谏书稀。</p>

</div>
