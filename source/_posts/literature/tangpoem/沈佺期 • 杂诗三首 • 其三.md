<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 沈佺期
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">闻道黄龙戍，频年不解兵。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">可怜闺里月，长在汉家营。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">少妇今春意，良人昨夜情。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">谁能将旗鼓，一为取龙城。</p>

</div>
