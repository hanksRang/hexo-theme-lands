<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张九龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">孤鸿海上来，池潢不敢顾。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">侧见双翠鸟，巢在三珠树。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">矫矫珍木巅，得无金丸惧？</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">美服患人指，高明逼神恶。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">今我游冥冥，弋者何所慕！</p>

</div>
