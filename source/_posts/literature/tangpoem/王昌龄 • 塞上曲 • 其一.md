<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王昌龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">蝉鸣空桑林，八月萧关道。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">出塞入塞寒，处处黄芦草。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">从来幽并客，皆共尘沙老。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">莫学游侠儿，矜夸紫骝好。</p>

</div>
