<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 常建
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">清晨入古寺，初日照高林。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">曲径通幽处，禅房花木深。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">山光悦鸟性，潭影空人心。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">万籁此俱寂，惟余钟磬音。</p>

</div>
