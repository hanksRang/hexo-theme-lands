<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">万壑树参天，千山响杜鹃。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">山中一夜雨，树杪百重泉。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">汉女输橦布，巴人讼芋田。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">文翁翻教授，不敢倚先贤。</p>

</div>
