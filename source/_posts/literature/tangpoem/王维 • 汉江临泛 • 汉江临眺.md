<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">楚塞三湘接，荆门九派通。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">江流天地外，山色有无中。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">郡邑浮前浦，波澜动远空。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">襄阳好风日，留醉与山翁。</p>

</div>
