<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">寒山转苍翠，秋水日潺湲。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">倚杖柴门外，临风听暮蝉。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">渡头馀落日，墟里上孤烟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">复值接舆醉，狂歌五柳前。</p>

</div>
