<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张九龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">幽人归独卧，滞虑洗孤清。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">持此谢高鸟，因之传远情。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">日夕怀空意，人谁感至精？</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">飞沈理自隔，何所慰吾诚?</p>

</div>
