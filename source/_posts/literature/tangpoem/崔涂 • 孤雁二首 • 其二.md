<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 崔涂
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">几行归塞尽，念尔独何之。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">暮雨相呼失，寒塘欲下迟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">渚云低暗度，关月冷相随。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">未必逢矰缴，孤飞自可疑。</p>

</div>
