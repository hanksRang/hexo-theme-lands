<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 司空曙
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">世乱同南去，时清独北还。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">他乡生白发，旧国见青山。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">晓月过残垒，繁星宿故关。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">寒禽与衰草，处处伴愁颜。</p>

</div>
