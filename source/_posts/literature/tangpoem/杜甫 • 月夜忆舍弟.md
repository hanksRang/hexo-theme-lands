<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">戍鼓断人行，边秋一雁声。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">露从今夜白，月是故乡明。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">有弟皆分散，无家问死生。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">寄书长不达，况乃未休兵。</p>

</div>
