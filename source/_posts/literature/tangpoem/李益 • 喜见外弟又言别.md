<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李益
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">十年离乱后，长大一相逢。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">问姓惊初见，称名忆旧容。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">别来沧海事，语罢暮天钟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">明日巴陵道，秋山又几重。</p>

</div>
