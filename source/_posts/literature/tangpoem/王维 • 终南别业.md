<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">中岁颇好道，晚家南山陲。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">兴来每独往，胜事空自知。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">行到水穷处，坐看云起时。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">偶然值林叟，谈笑无还期。</p>

</div>
