<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 常建
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">清溪深不测，隐处唯孤云。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">松际露微月，清光犹为君。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">茅亭宿花影，药院滋苔纹。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">余亦谢时去，西山鸾鹤群。</p>

</div>
