<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">昔闻洞庭水，今上岳阳楼。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">吴楚东南坼，乾坤日夜浮。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">亲朋无一字，老病有孤舟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">戎马关山北，凭轩涕泗流。</p>

</div>
