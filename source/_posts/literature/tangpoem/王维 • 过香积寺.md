<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王维
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">不知香积寺，数里入云峰。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">古木无人径，深山何处钟。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">泉声咽危石，日色冷青松。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">薄暮空潭曲，安禅制毒龙。</p>

</div>
