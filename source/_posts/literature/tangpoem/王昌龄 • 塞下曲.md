<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王昌龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">饮马渡秋水，水寒风似刀。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">平沙日未没，黯黯见临洮。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">昔日长城战，咸言意气高。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">黄尘足今古，白骨乱蓬蒿。</p>

</div>
