<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">明朝驿使发，一夜絮征袍。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">素手抽针冷，那堪把剪刀。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">裁缝寄远道，几日到临洮。</p>

</div>
