<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 司空曙
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">静夜四无邻，荒居旧业贫。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">雨中黄叶树，灯下白头人。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">以我独沉久，愧君相见频。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">平生自有分，况是蔡家亲。</p>

</div>
