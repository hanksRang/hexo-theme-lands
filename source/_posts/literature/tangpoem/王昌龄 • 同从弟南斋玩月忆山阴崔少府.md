<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王昌龄
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">高卧南斋时，开帷月初吐。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">清辉澹水木，演漾在窗户。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">冉冉几盈虚，澄澄变今古。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">美人清江畔，是夜越吟苦。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">千里共如何，微风吹兰杜。</p>

</div>
