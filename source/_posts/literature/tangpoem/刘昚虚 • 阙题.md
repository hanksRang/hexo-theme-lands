<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 刘昚虚
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">道由白云尽，春与青溪长。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">时有落花至，远随流水香。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">闲门向山路，深柳读书堂。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">幽映每白日，清辉照衣裳。</p>

</div>
