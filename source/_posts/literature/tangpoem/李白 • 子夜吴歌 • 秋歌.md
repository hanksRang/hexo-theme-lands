<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">长安一片月，万户捣衣声。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">秋风吹不尽，总是玉关情。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">何日平胡虏，良人罢远征。</p>

</div>
