<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟郊
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">慈母手中线，游子身上衣。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">临行密密缝，意恐迟迟归。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">谁言寸草心，报得三春晖。</p>

</div>
