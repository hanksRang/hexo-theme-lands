<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 许浑
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">红叶晚萧萧，长亭酒一瓢。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">残云归太华，疏雨过中条。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">树色随山迥，河声入海遥。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">帝乡明日到，犹自梦渔樵。</p>

</div>
