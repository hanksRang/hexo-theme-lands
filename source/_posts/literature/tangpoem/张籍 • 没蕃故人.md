<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 张籍
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">前年伐月支，城下没全师。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">蕃汉断消息，死生长别离。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">无人收废帐，归马识残旗。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欲祭疑君在，天涯哭此时。</p>

</div>
