<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 杜甫
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">凉风起天末，君子意如何。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">鸿雁几时到，江湖秋水多。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">文章憎命达，魑魅喜人过。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">应共冤魂语，投诗赠汨罗。</p>

</div>
