<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 温庭筠
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">荒戍落黄叶，浩然离故关。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">高风汉阳渡，初日郢门山。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">江上几人在，天涯孤棹还。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">何当重相见，樽酒慰离颜。</p>

</div>
