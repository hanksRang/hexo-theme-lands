<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">吾爱孟夫子，风流天下闻。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">红颜弃轩冕，白首卧松云。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">醉月频中圣，迷花不事君。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">高山安可仰，徒此揖清芬。</p>

</div>
