<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 孟浩然
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">北山白云里，隐者自怡悦。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">相望试登高，心随雁飞灭。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">愁因薄暮起，兴是清秋发。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">时见归村人，沙行渡头歇。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">天边树若荠，江畔洲如月。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">何当载酒来，共醉重阳节。</p>

</div>
