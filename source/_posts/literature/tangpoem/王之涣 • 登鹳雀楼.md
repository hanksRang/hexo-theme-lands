<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 王之涣
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">白日依山尽，黄河入海流。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">欲穷千里目，更上一层楼。</p>

</div>
