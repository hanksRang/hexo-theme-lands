<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">秦地罗敷女，采桑绿水边。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">素手青条上，红妆白日鲜。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">蚕饥妾欲去，五马莫留连。</p>

</div>
