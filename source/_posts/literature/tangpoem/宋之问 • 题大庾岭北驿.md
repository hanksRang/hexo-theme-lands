<div id="poem-author">
唐代 • 宋之问
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">阳月南飞雁，传闻至此回。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">我行殊未已，何日复归来。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">江静潮初落，林昏瘴不开。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">明朝望乡处，应见陇头梅。</p>

</div>
