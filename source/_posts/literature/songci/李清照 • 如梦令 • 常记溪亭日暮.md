<div id="poem-author">
    宋代 • 李清照
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">常记溪亭日暮，沉醉不知归路。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">兴尽晚回舟，误入藕花深处。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">争渡，争渡，惊起一滩鸥鹭。</p>

</div>
