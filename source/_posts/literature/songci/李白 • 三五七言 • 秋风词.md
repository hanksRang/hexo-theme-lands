<div id="poem-author">
    唐代 • 李白
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">秋风清，秋月明，</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">落叶聚还散，寒鸦栖复惊。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">相思相见知何日？此时此夜难为情！</p>

</div>
