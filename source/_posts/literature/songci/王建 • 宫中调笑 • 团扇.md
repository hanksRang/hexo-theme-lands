<div id="poem-author">
    唐代 • 王建
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">团扇，团扇，美人病来遮面。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">玉颜憔悴三年，谁复商量管弦。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">弦管，弦管，春草昭阳路断。</p>

</div>
