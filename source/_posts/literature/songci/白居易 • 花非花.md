<div id="poem-author">
    唐代 • 白居易
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">花非花，雾非雾。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">夜半来，天明去。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">来如春梦几多时？</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">去似朝云无觅处。</p>

</div>
