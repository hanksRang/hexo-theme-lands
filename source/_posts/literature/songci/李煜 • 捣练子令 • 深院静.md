<div id="poem-author">
    五代 • 李煜
</div>
<div id="poem-body">
<p class="poem-paragraph">深院静，小庭空，断续寒砧断续风。</p>
<p class="poem-paragraph">无奈夜长人不寐，数声和月到帘栊。</p>

</div>
